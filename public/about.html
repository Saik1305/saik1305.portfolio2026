Hello World 🌍

My name is Alessio Cicciarella and I’m a 24-year-old graphic designer based in Stuttgart.

My practice concentrates on combining theoretical ideas with design. The questions I’m thinking about are (new) media, philosophy and film studies 🎬

For my apprenticeship I worked at a small screenprint manufacture in Hamburg from October 2024 to March 2025.

I love to read and write poetry/music. In my spare time, I like to practice the art of living in an analog world — writing with fountain pens and listening to CDs. (And I am also an avid collector, naturally…)

If that sounds interesting to you, come and say hello 😊
